🔒 Privacy Policy

How Bolt handles data. Last updated February 2026.

Summary

Bolt does not create user accounts, track individuals, or sell data. Serial number lookups run entirely in your browser and are never transmitted to our servers. Product search queries are sent to Google's AI API to generate results and are briefly cached to improve performance.

What We Collect

Serial number lookups ("How Old is my Item?"): For supported brands, all decoding logic runs entirely in your browser using local JavaScript. No serial number you enter is transmitted to Bolt's servers or any third party.

Product search queries (Item Lookup): When you use the Item Lookup search, your query text is sent to our server API, which forwards it to Google's Gemini AI service to generate a research report. The query may also be stored briefly in a server-side cache (see Data Retention below).

Brand + model lookups: If you use the alternative lookup (brand and model number without a serial), your input is sent to the same server API and AI service.

We do not collect your name, email address, IP address beyond standard server logs, or any identifying information unless you voluntarily submit it through the contact form.

How Data Is Used

Query data is used solely to generate and return a research result to you. Cached results are reused when the same or similar query is received within the cache window to reduce API costs and improve response speed. We do not analyze queries in aggregate to build profiles or sell data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Bolt relies on the following external services. When you use the Item Lookup feature, your query may reach these providers:

  • Google Gemini API (Google LLC) — Receives your product search query or model lookup text to generate AI-powered research results. Data is processed on Google's servers subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Upstash Redis (Upstash, Inc.) — Receives a cache key derived from your normalized query string to store and retrieve cached results. The key contains the query text. Data is stored subject to Upstash's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel (Vercel, Inc.) — Hosts this application and processes all HTTP requests. Vercel may retain standard server access logs (IP address, request path, timestamp) subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts (Google LLC) — Serves the Inter typeface used throughout the site. Your browser contacts Google's servers to download the font file; Google may log the request.

No analytics scripts, advertising trackers, or social media pixels are loaded on this site.

Data Retention

Search query results are cached in Upstash Redis with a time-to-live (TTL) of 15 minutes (900 seconds). After expiration, cached data is automatically deleted from Upstash's servers. We do not maintain a permanent query log or database of user submissions.

Cookies & Local Storage

Bolt does not set cookies. No persistent data is stored in your browser's local storage or session storage by this application.

Children's Privacy

This service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please use our contact form. We will respond to data inquiries as promptly as possible.
