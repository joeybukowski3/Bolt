Who We Are

Bolt was built by active insurance industry professionals who understand the daily challenges adjusters face. From estimating replacement costs to determining actual cash value, our team has spent years in the trenches handling claims, writing estimates, and navigating the complexities of property and casualty insurance. We created Bolt because we saw a need for a tool that truly speaks the language of adjusters.

By combining decades of real-world claims experience with the latest advances in artificial intelligence, Bolt delivers fast, accurate product research tailored specifically for the insurance workflow. Our mission is to save adjusters time, reduce errors, and bring transparency to every claim — all powered by technology designed by the people who understand it best.
