Why Bolt?

Searching Google is a starting point. Bolt is a finish line.

Built for the Job

⚡

Structured, Not Scattered

Instead of running five different searches and hoping you find the right answer, Bolt runs them automatically and surfaces what matters in one place.

✓

Conclusions, Not Just Links

Google hands you a list of pages. Bolt hands you actionable information a report writer can use immediately.

◈

Consistent Every Time

Every user, every search, same disciplined process. No more variation in results based on how someone happens to phrase a query that day.

✗

Inconsistent Research Eliminated

Google search results are heavily reliant on prior search history and predictive algorithms, creating inconsistency among users. Bolt provides a consistent, company-minded framework for every user.

LKQ Evaluations Made Simple

Collecting relevant data is critical in making LKQ decisions. Bolt provides a clean framework for users to make informed decisions in an easy-to-access format that promotes user preference.

Highly Expandable

As technology advances, so will Bolt. The smarter AI tools become, the smarter our product becomes. We lead the charge in utilizing data aggregation methods and information analytics.

📝

Enhanced Virtual Reports

Ability to build in field data points from alternative sources that help generate actionable item reports remotely — an additional service offering with reduced cost and time constraints.
