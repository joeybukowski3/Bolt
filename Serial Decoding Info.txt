  4. Electronics (electronics.csv)


    1 "Brand","Decoding Method"
    2 "Apple","12-char serial: char 4 = Year, chars 5-6 = Week. 10-char
      serial (post-2021): randomized."
    3 "ASUS","Char 1 = Year (alpha code), char 2 = Month (1-9 = Jan-Sep,
      A=Oct, B=Nov, C=Dec)."
    4 "Google Pixel","Char 1 = Year last digit, chars 2-3 = Week number
      (01-53)."
    5 "HP","Char 4 = Year last digit, chars 5-6 = Week number (01-52)."
    6 "LG Electronics","Character 1"
    7 "Panasonic","Char 1 = Year last digit. Char 2 = Month or factory
      code (mapping varies by product line)."
    8 "Samsung (Phones & Tablets)","15-char serial: char 8 = Year.
      Shorter serials: char 4 = Year. Next char = Month."
    9 "Samsung Electronics","Char 8 (15-digit serial) or Char 4
      (11-digit serial)"
   10 "Sony","Last letter of MODEL number encodes year: H=2020, J=2021,
      K=2022, L=2023, M=2024, N=2025."
   11 "Vizio","Chars 4-5 = Year/Week code. Decoded using YYWW and WWYY
      heuristics; verify with model info."
