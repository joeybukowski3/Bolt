Accuracy Limitations

Serial number formats vary by brand, product line, and production era. Many manufacturers reuse year codes on repeating cycles of 10 to 30 years, meaning a single serial number may correspond to multiple possible manufacture years. While we flag these cases and assign a confidence level, we cannot guarantee the result will always match the actual manufacture date of the specific unit.

Our database is maintained to the best of our ability but may not reflect every serial format variation, regional manufacturing difference, or mid-cycle format change introduced by a manufacturer.

AI-Assisted Results

The Alternative Lookup feature uses an AI language model to estimate product age when no serial number is available. AI-generated results are estimates based on general product knowledge and publicly available information. They are more likely to contain errors than direct serial number decodes and should be treated as a starting point for further research, not a definitive answer.
