ⓘ How to Use This Tool

Quick Decoding

  1. Click Appliances or Water Heaters
  2. Choose the brand from the dropdown
  3. If an Era selector appears, choose Pre-2006 or Post-2006
  4. Enter the serial number exactly as shown on the label
  5. Click Decode Serial Number

No Serial Number?

Use the Smart Lookup section. Enter a model number, brand name, or description (e.g., "GE Profile PFE28KSKSS") to get an AI-powered age estimate.

Finding Serial Numbers

  • Refrigerators: Inside on the left or right wall
  • Ovens/Ranges: Inside the door or on the back panel
  • Dishwashers: Top edge of the door or side panel
  • Washers/Dryers: Inside the door or on the back
  • Water Heaters: On a sticker/plate on the side of the tank
For Insurance Claims: Many serial number codes repeat every 10–20 years. Always verify the decade using physical condition, model features, purchase receipts, or installation records. Document which method you used to determine the final manufacture date.
