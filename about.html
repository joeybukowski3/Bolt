Our Story

The Serial Number Decoder was created by a team of licensed insurance adjusters, independent appraisers, and appliance technicians who shared a common frustration: there was no single, reliable resource for quickly establishing the manufacture date of an appliance or water heater during a claims investigation.

We built what we wished had existed — a clean, fast, professional-grade tool that consolidates decades of manufacturer serial number documentation into one place.

Why It Matters

Accurate manufacture dates are critical to fair, defensible claim outcomes. Our team uses this tool daily for:

💵
Actual Cash Value
Calculating ACV based on verified age and expected useful life.
📋
Warranty Verification
Confirming whether a unit falls within manufacturer warranty periods.
⚖
Claim Documentation
Supporting defensible, well-documented age determinations in claim files.
⏱
Faster Settlements
Eliminating back-and-forth with manufacturers and tech support lines.

Our Database

Our decoder database is compiled from manufacturer technical documentation, service manuals, and trusted industry references including electrical-forensics.com and homespy.io. It is reviewed and updated regularly to keep pace with evolving serial number formats across appliance and water heater brands.

Who This Is For

This tool is designed for insurance adjusters, independent appraisers, field technicians, contractors, and other claims professionals who need fast, reliable manufacture date information. It is freely accessible and provided as a professional resource to the industry.

For formal claim use: Results should always be corroborated with supporting documentation — installation records, purchase receipts, or physical inspection — when used in claim proceedings. Serial number formats can repeat across decades; always verify the correct era before finalizing a determination.
